Title: Restless Leg Syndrome (RLS)
Description: Restless Leg Syndrome is a condition that causes an uncontrollable urge to move the legs, usually due to discomfort. It typically occurs in the evening or night while sitting or lying down and is temporarily relieved by movement.
Symptoms:
- Urge to move the legs, especially at night
- Relief with movement such as walking or stretching
- Difficulty falling or staying asleep
- Nighttime leg twitching or jerking
Treatment:
- Iron supplements (if iron deficiency is present)
- Medications affecting dopamine levels
- Avoiding caffeine, alcohol, and nicotine
- Warm baths, leg massages, and using heating pads
