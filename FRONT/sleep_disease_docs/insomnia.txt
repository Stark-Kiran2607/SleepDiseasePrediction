Title: Insomnia
Description: Insomnia is a common sleep disorder that makes it hard to fall asleep, stay asleep, or causes early waking and inability to return to sleep.
Symptoms:
- Difficulty falling asleep at night
- Waking up during the night
- Feeling tired after a night's sleep
- Irritability and daytime fatigue
Treatment:
- Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT-I)
- Relaxation techniques
- Prescription medications
- Avoiding caffeine and screen time before bed
