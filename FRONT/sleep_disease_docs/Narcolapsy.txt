Title: Narcolepsy
Description: Narcolepsy is a chronic neurological disorder that affects the brain's ability to control sleep-wake cycles.
Symptoms:
- Excessive daytime sleepiness
- Sudden loss of muscle tone (cataplexy)
- Sleep paralysis
- Hallucinations while falling asleep or waking
Treatment:
- Stimulant medications
- Scheduled naps
- Antidepressants
- Lifestyle adjustments to improve alertness
