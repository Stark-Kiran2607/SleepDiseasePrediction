Title: Sleep Apnea
Description: Sleep apnea is a potentially serious sleep disorder where breathing repeatedly stops and starts.
Symptoms:
- Loud snoring
- Episodes of stopped breathing during sleep
- Gasping for air during sleep
- Morning headache and daytime drowsiness
Treatment:
- CPAP (Continuous Positive Airway Pressure) therapy
- Weight loss and lifestyle changes
- Surgery (in severe cases)
- Oral appliances
